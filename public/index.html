Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need to change my phone number?
No. You keep your existing business number on all your marketing, signs, and Google listing. We set up a dedicated AI number, and your existing line forwards to it when you don't answer (or 24/7 if you prefer). Customers never know anything changed on your end.
How long does setup take?
5-7 business days from signup to fully live. We handle everything: AI personality tuning to match your business, integration with your existing number, voice configuration, and full testing. You fill out a short onboarding form and we do the rest.
Will it really sound like my business?
Yes. During onboarding you tell us your business name, tone of voice, pricing guidance, booking rules, and anything the AI should never say. We train it on your specifics so customers feel like they're talking to your team — not a generic chatbot.
What if the AI gets something wrong?
You see every conversation in real time and can step in anytime. The AI also knows when to escalate — if a caller asks something it can't handle, it transfers them to you (or takes a message). You stay in full control and can review every call in your dashboard.
What happens if I'm on vacation for a week?
Your AI handles every call. You come back to a dashboard full of qualified leads with full transcripts, instead of a pile of missed calls and lost revenue.
Can I see all the conversations?
Yes. Your dashboard shows every call with full transcripts, recordings, lead score, and pipeline status. You can search by phone number, filter by date, and export everything to CSV anytime.
Is my customer data safe?
Yes. Conversations are encrypted in transit and at rest, data is stored securely on US-based infrastructure, and we never share or sell any customer information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no annual commitments. Cancel anytime by emailing us. Plus our 30-day money-back guarantee — if you're not happy in the first month, we refund you in full.
