Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Can the AI actually answer the phone, not just text?
Yes — that's what the Pro tier is for. The AI picks up live calls and has a natural voice conversation with the caller. It knows your pricing, hours, services, and can book appointments during the call. Voice AI configuration takes 5-7 days after signup so we can tune it specifically to your business.
How long does setup really take?
48 hours for Starter (text-only AI). 5-7 days for Pro (includes voice AI configuration). We handle everything: phone number registration with carriers, AI personality tuning, integration with your existing number, and testing. You just fill out a short onboarding form and we do the rest.
Will it really sound like my business?
Yes. During onboarding you tell us your business name, tone of voice, pricing guidance, booking rules, and anything the AI should never say. We train it on your specifics so customers feel like they're talking to your team — not a generic chatbot.
Do I need a new phone number?
No. We set up a dedicated AI number that forwards to your existing line when you're available. Missed calls get the AI treatment automatically. Your customers never know anything changed on your end.
What if the AI messes up?
You get notified for every conversation and can jump in anytime. The AI also knows when to escalate to a human and will transfer calls automatically if the situation needs it. You stay in full control and can review every conversation in your dashboard.
What happens if I'm on vacation for a week?
Your AI handles everything — calls get answered, texts get responded to, appointments get booked into your calendar for when you return. You come back to a full schedule instead of a pile of missed calls.
Can I see all the conversations?
Yes. Your dashboard shows every call, text, and appointment — with full transcripts, caller info, and booking status. You can see exactly what the AI said, what the customer said, and what was booked.
Is my customer data safe?
Yes. Conversations are encrypted, data is stored securely on US-based infrastructure, and we never share or sell any customer information. We're fully TCPA and A2P 10DLC compliant — see our Privacy Policy for full details.
