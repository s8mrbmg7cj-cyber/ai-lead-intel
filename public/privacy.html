Who we are

AI Lead Intel ("we," "us," or "our") provides an AI-powered missed-call recovery and customer communication service for small businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our services or interact with our website at aileadintel.com.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information we collect

Information you provide directly

When you sign up, onboard, or use our services, we collect:

  • Contact information: name, phone number, email address
  • Business information: business name (DBA), legal business name, EIN/tax ID, business address, entity type, industry, website URL
  • Service configuration: business hours, call handling preferences, communication tone, sample messages, payment/booking links
  • Communications: messages you send us, support requests, feedback

Information collected automatically

When you use our services, we automatically collect:

  • Usage data: pages visited, time on site, clicks, form submissions
  • Device data: browser type, operating system, IP address, device identifiers
  • Call & message metadata: call duration, transcripts, voicemail recordings, SMS delivery status (for the services we provide to you)

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our services
  • Register your business with telecommunications carriers for SMS compliance (A2P 10DLC)
  • Process payments and manage your account
  • Send you service-related communications, including onboarding updates, account notifications, and support responses
  • Respond to your inquiries, questions, and support requests
  • Improve our services, troubleshoot issues, and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect the security and integrity of our services

SMS/text messaging privacy

Phone numbers collected through our website forms or inbound communications are used exclusively to communicate directly with you regarding your inquiry, account, and services from AI Lead Intel.

We DO NOT sell, rent, share, or transfer your phone number, SMS consent information, or opt-in data to any third party or affiliate for marketing purposes. Your mobile opt-in data will not be shared with anyone for any purpose, including affiliates or partners.

When you opt in to SMS

By providing your phone number and checking the SMS consent box on our forms, you consent to receive automated and non-automated text messages from AI Lead Intel. These may include:

  • Onboarding confirmations and updates
  • Service notifications (e.g., when your system goes live)
  • Account alerts (e.g., billing reminders, service status)
  • Support responses to your inquiries

Message frequency and rates

Message frequency varies based on your activity with AI Lead Intel. Message and data rates may apply. Standard carrier rates for your mobile plan will apply to all messages sent and received.

How to opt out

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. Once you opt out, you will receive no further SMS messages from us.

Reply HELP to any message for support, or contact us at hello@aileadintel.com.

Re-subscription

If you have opted out and wish to resume receiving messages, reply START to our number, or contact our support team.

Cookies & tracking

Our website uses essential cookies to maintain basic functionality (e.g., remembering form progress, session state). We do not use cookies for advertising or third-party tracking.

Your browser's onboarding form data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage, so your progress is saved if you close the tab. This data never leaves your device until you submit the form.
